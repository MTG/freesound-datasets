Our vision ...


Transparency

The datasets creation process should be as transparent as possible to easily spot possible faults

Openness

Data should be released under open licenses to facilitate its distribution and reproducible research

Dynamism

Datasets should be dynamic in the sense that they can evolve over time in quality and quantity

... and our plan!


Freesound content

We are leveraging Freesound.org (>360k sounds) as a source of open audio content for creating datasets

Community-based

We rely on the community to sustainably crowdsource sound annotations and to improve our platform

The FreesoundDatasets platform

Facilitates annotation gathering, data exploration, downloading, faults reporting and the discussion around the dataset



Explore, download and contribute to the FreesoundDataset

FreesoundDataset (FSD) is a large-scale, general-purpose dataset composed of Freesound.org content organised into the AudioSet Ontology.
