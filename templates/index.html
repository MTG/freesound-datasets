Welcome to the Freesound Datasets platform!

Freesound Datasets is a platform for the collaborative creation of open audio datasets based on Freesound content. We believe that datasets can have a greater impact if they are created and maintained according to some principles. In our view, datasets should be transparent in their creation process, should be released under open licenses to allow their free distribution, and should be dynamic in the sense that they can evolve over time in quality and quantity. Freesound Datasets facilitates the creation and maintenance of audio datasets which are built with these principles in mind.

How?

We rely on the community to make this project sustainable. We leverage Freesound.org as source of open audio content for the creation of datasets. We plan to gather sound annotations through crowdsourcing using Freesound Datasets in order to construct ground truth for the datasets.

What can be done in this platform?

You can explore the content of the hosted datasets and you can also download them. But even more importantly, you can contribute to their creation in different ways. Currently, you can annotate sound in order to generate ground truth and you can discuss the datasets as well as the platform. In the future we plan to enable more functionalities, such as the reporting of mislabeled or faulty audio samples.

You can find more information in our ISMIR 2017 paper. You can also visit our FAQ page. If you wish to share comments or suggestions, please visit our Discussion page, or subscribe to our Google Group: FreesoundDatasets to stay up-to-date about this platform and hosted datasets. If you use this platform or any of the hosted datasets in academic work please cite:
E. Fonseca, J. Pons, X. Favory, F. Font, D. Bogdanov, A. Ferraro, S. Oramas, A. Porter and X. Serra. “Freesound Datasets: A Platform for the Creation of Open Audio Datasets” In Proceedings of the 18th International Society for Music Information Retrieval Conference, Suzhou, China, 2017.

We plan to host several open audio datasets built out of Freesound content. The first and most important dataset is called FreesoundDataset (FSD).

FreesoundDataset (FSD) is a large-scale, general-purpose dataset composed of Freesound content organised into the ontology of Google's AudioSet.
