Welcome to the Freesound Datasets platform

The goal of Freesound Datasets is to have a website to host and create datasets based on Freesound content. The website should allow collaborative creation of public datasets and should provide a way for researchers to explore the dataset and to download it. By “exploring the dataset” we mean something like Google AudioSet’s site. Although the project should be generic enough to support several datasets, the most important dataset is called FreesoundDataset.

You can find more information in our ISMIR 2017 paper.

The Dataset:

FreesoundDataset

FreesoundDataset is basically a classification of Freesound content into the ontology that Google use in AudioSet.
