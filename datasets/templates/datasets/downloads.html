FSDKaggle2018 FSDKaggle2018 is an audio dataset containing 11,073 audio files annotated with 41 labels of the AudioSet Ontology. It has been used for the DCASE Challenge 2018 Task 2, which was run as a Kaggle competition titled Freesound General-Purpose Audio Tagging Challenge.
FSDnoisy18k FSDnoisy18k is an audio dataset collected with the aim of fostering the investigation of label noise in sound event classification. It contains 42.5 hours of audio across 20 sound classes, including a small amount of manually-labeled data and a larger quantity of real-world noisy data.
